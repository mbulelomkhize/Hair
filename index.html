Our Story

Where Quality
Meets Confidence.

Cherry Crown Hair was born from one conviction: every woman deserves a crown that fits — beautifully, affordably, and delivered with genuine care. Based in Hammarsdale, KwaZulu-Natal, we source only the finest lace frontals and wigs so you always step out looking your absolute best.

From voluminous jerry curls to sleek straight styles, every piece is selected for quality, density, and a natural-looking finish. And because we believe in going the extra mile, every single order includes a complimentary hair supply gift — at no extra cost.
